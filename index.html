Best drawings

Black sharpie on bright white paper. Bold lines, no sketchy strokes. Closed shapes make the best cutters.

Even lighting, no shadows across the drawing. Camera parallel to the paper — avoid angled shots.

Print settings

PLA works great. 0.2mm layer height, 3+ perimeters for strong walls. No supports needed.
